In one sentence
AEI is a persistent intelligence layer for the highest-stakes decisions a company makes — capital, M&A, transformation, portfolio. The Bloomberg Terminal for strategic decisions.

Aera Technology just proved that operational decision intelligence is venture-scale — named a Gartner Leader in January 2026. But the strategic quadrant — the quarterly, board-level decisions — is still empty. AEI claims it before it gets named. That timing is the whole bet.

How it works · in four steps
1
Reads your systems
Read-only, from the tools where strategy actually happens — ERP, planning, HR, CRM, billing. Nothing re-keyed.
2
Watches the market
Filings, analysts, patents, peers — the outside view that keeps the score honest and peer-relative.
3
Scores the genotype
A 14-agent engine scores the company across six dimensions, every week, with a confidence level and a reasoning trail.
4
Ships bound decisions
STOP / RESEQUENCE / ACQUIRE — each with a € value and an audit trail — delivered into the board’s own cadence.
